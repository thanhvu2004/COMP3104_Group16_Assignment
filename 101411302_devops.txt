The COMP 3104 DevOps course at George Brown College focuses on the principles and practices of DevOps, emphasizing collaboration, automation, and continuous delivery. Students learn about tools and methodologies used in modern software development, including version control systems (like Git), CI/CD pipelines, containerization (using Docker), and configuration management. The course aims to bridge the gap between development and operations teams, enabling efficient and automated software deployment processes. It typically includes hands-on labs and projects to provide real-world experience with DevOps practices.
